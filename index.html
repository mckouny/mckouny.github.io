Profiling users with .bashhistory

Does our bash behaviour exhibit distinctive patterns that can identify individuals?

Cyber attribution is hard. Analyzing the patterns in commands used by an attacker, we may be able to link different attacks to him, or at least to someone who had the same training.

For my dissertation, I collect (anonymized) .bashhistory files from many users and use some ML™ to link them together. For that, I need a lot of data and I would appreciate if you shared your history with me.
To see how I handle your data securely and anonymously, read on.
